    • Other Programs and Competitions

    Explore a variety of math resources designed to challenge and inspire mathematicians of all levels. Discover opportunities to enhance your skills, connect with peers, and show your talents!

    • Art of Problem Solving

      A comprehensive platform offering textbooks, online courses, and problem-solving tools tailored for aspiring mathematicians. AoPS emphasizes creative thinking and advanced problem-solving skills, making it a go-to resource for students preparing for competitions like the AMC and AIME. Its vibrant online community also connects learners with peers and mentors worldwide.

    • Khan Academy

      A free educational platform offering video tutorials, interactive exercises, and comprehensive courses for a wide range of topics, including foundational and advanced mathematics. Khan Academy makes learning accessible and personalized for students to work at their own pace. Its extensive resources also prepare students for standardized tests and competitive math.

    • Minnesota MATHCOUNTS

      A middle school math program that combines coaching, practice, and competitions to inspire a love of problem-solving. Minnesota MATHCOUNTS offers a pathway for young mathematicians to improve their skills while collaborating with peers. The program culminates in exciting competitions at local, state, and national levels.

    • Minnesota State High School Mathematics League

      The Minnesota State High School Math League exists to identify students with exceptional interest and/or mathematical ability, give them recognition and encouragement, bring them together with similarly motivated students, and introduce them to topics not commonly taught in the high school curriculum.

    • Art of Sciences

      The 2025 “Art of Sciences” Summer Camp will be organized by RMC Academy, LLC. Our goal is to inspire students to pursue various STEM fields, including math, physics, biology, computer science, machine learning and AI, creative sound design, as well as economics, business, reading, photography, and baking.

    • Rochester Math Club

      Rochester Math Club is a non-profit organization consisting of students, coaches and parents who are dedicated to inspire a mathematical mindset in the Rochester community. Our ultimate goal is to provide math-driven students with an opportunity to expand their understandings of complex mathematical topics.

    • Math + Science Club

      A student-run 501(c)3 non-profit organization to increase students' interest in competition math and science. Weekly club meetings are designed primarily for middle and high school students, featuring math, physics, and computer science lectures and science bowl practice. Additionally, the organization hosts events such as scientist roundtables, tournaments, STEM parties, and student panels.

    • LIVE, by Po-Shen Loh

      The first and only online math course that captivates, entertains, and inspires. Invented by legendary math coach Po-Shen Loh. Taught by instructors who are not only brilliant at math but also skilled in performance arts. Sign up for a trial class to shatter your expectations for mathematics at https://live.poshenloh.com/trial-class.

    • Athemath

      The Athemath program is a mix of both math and community-building, where you do a lot of math and also get to know some other wonderful people while you're at it. Their mission is to both provide high-quality, low-cost contest math classes for ambitious girls as well as create a community of math girls, from all levels of success, age, and experience, that can support each other on their math journey.

    • Mathandai4girls

      The Math and AI Girls Competition aims to encourage young girls to develop an interest in math and AI by taking part in STEM competitions at an early age. This competition also aims to encourage long-term planning of academic and career goals in STEM. The competition is open to middle school female students. Award recipients are selected based on their aptitudes, activities and aspirations in STEM.

    • AwesomeMath

      A rigorous summer program for mathematically gifted middle and high school students, focusing on advanced problem-solving and competition preparation. Each 3-week session challenges students to think critically and engage in collaboration with other motivated peers.

    • MathPath

      A residential summer program for middle schoolers who demonstrate exceptional mathematical ability and curiosity. MathPath introduces students to advanced topics and problem-solving techniques, often through fun and engaging formats.

    • MathILy

      An intensive summer program for high school students passionate about math, combining challenging coursework with an exploratory approach. MathILy is designed for students who are creative and enthusiastic in their approaches to learning, creating an environment for learning, fun, and personal growth.

    • Canada/USA Mathcamp

      A transformative summer experience for mathematically talented high school students, offering courses, unique project opportunities, and a vibrant community. Canada/USA Mathcamp allows participants to design their own curriculum, choosing from a wide range of topics and difficulty levels.
