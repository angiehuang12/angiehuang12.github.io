    • About Us

    We are a group of passionate students that seeks to share our experiences in competition math. Minnesota is still a relatively under-resourced state in competitive math education, having significantly less participation in state and national competitions. We hope to inspire more students to excel at competition math and help others along the way.

    Founder

    Angie Huang

    Angie has been involved in competition math since 5th grade, having participated in a variety of math competitions such as ARML, PUMAC, and Girls in Math at Yale. She is a captain of the Minnesota All-State Math Team. This year, she received an Honorable Mention on the USAJMO and competed at the Math Prize for Girls. Besides math, she enjoys congressional debate, piano, and tennis and considers herself to be a die-heart football fan.

    Instructors

    • Aarya Garimella

      Aarya has been involved with competition math since 8th grade, and he has qualified for AIME. He is a captain for the Minnesota All-State Math Team, participating in competitions like ARML and CMIMC with the team. He also enjoys coaching his local middle school's MATHCOUNTS team. When not partaking in math, Aarya can be found listening to music or playing chess (or doing both).

    • Jinu Lee

      Jinu (Jaden) has been involved in various math competitions such as ARML, SMT, HMMT, and MathCounts. He’s a Captain of the Minnesota All-State Math Team. Additionally, he competed in MathCounts Nationals with Team MN in 2021 and has qualified for the AIME several times. Outside of math, Jinu enjoys volunteering, computer science, speech, debate, and cello.
