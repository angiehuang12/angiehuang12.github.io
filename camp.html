    • MNYMO Camp 2025

    Over 120 students joined us last year—now it’s your turn! Our free 12-day online camp runs weekdays August 11th–26th, 1–4 PM. Register here!

    Camp Overview

    Looking for a dynamic summer experience to improve your competition math skills? Join Minnesota Youth Math Outreach for our free online camp! Led by members of the Minnesota All-State Math Team (including this year’s captains!), you’ll dive deep into Algebra, Combinatorics, Number Theory, and Geometry. Each topic gets three days of hands-on lessons, starting with core concepts and moving into advanced problem-solving techniques like Vieta’s formulas, multiplicative order, the Chinese remainder theorem, recursion, Diophantine equations, and mass points. All students are welcome—middle schoolers in particular will find this a great way to build confidence before high-school competitions! We can’t wait to see you there!

    Schedule & Format

    • Online Camp: Weekdays, August 11th–26th, 1–4 PM via Zoom
    • Groups: Two skill-based tracks
      • Basics Track: Foundational concepts & introductory problem‐solving
      • Advanced Track: Challenging problems & advanced topics
    • Cost: Free for all participants

    Who Should Attend

    Anyone is welcome, but we especially encourage middle-schoolers to join! See our diagnostic problems to help evaluate if MNYMO is right for you and choose which group you anticipate joining.
