    • MNYMO Camp

    Over 120 students joined us for our online camp from August 12th to 27th! Students chose between two class groups based on their familiarity with concepts. Students learned concepts from the four main subject areas of competition math (Algebra, Combinatorics, Number Theory, and Geometry), each of which was covered with one lesson on fundamentals and two lessons on advanced topics (inequalities and complex numbers for Algebra, perspectives and recursion for Combinatorics, theorems and tricks for Number Theory, and mass points and trigonometry for Geometry). We had so much fun with group problem solving sessions and activities!
